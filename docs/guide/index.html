Getting Started

Your Complete Setup Guide

From choosing a plan to monitoring your KPIs — everything you need to get your AI CBO running and delivering results.

01

Choose Your Plan

Select the plan that matches your needs. Starter includes Blog Publisher, Facebook, and Instagram. Growth adds LinkedIn, Email Marketing, Prospector, SEO Analyst, and CRM Pipeline. Enterprise adds Voice Outreach and removes all content limits. Not sure yet? Start a 7-day free trial on the Starter plan — no credit card required.

TIP

All plans include the 90-day guarantee, a real-time dashboard, content calendar, and prospect CRM. You can upgrade at any time. Free trial users get full Starter plan access for 7 days.

View Pricing →
02

Complete the CBO Setup Form

After payment, you'll fill out a short setup form that takes about three minutes. This is how you brief your agent on your business. You can re-edit this form at any time from Agent Settings in your dashboard.

  • Business description — what you do and who your customers are
  • Website URL + ideal clients — AI builds a structured Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) for you
  • Content tone — professional, casual, educational, inspirational, or bold
  • Competitors to monitor (optional)
  • Telegram handle & ID for direct communication (optional but recommended)
  • Timezone — all dashboard timestamps adjust to your local time

After submitting, head to the Integrations page to connect the platforms you want your agent to manage — blog, social media, email, SEO, and more. Each module has a step-by-step setup guide.

TIP

Be specific. "B2B SaaS for DevOps teams at enterprises over 500 employees" outperforms "tech companies". The AI-powered ICP builder helps you nail this.

03

Connect Telegram

Your agent communicates with you through a private Telegram bot. After setup, the team configures your dedicated bot and pairs it with your account.

  • You receive a Telegram pairing code via email
  • Open Telegram, find your bot, and enter the pairing code
  • Your agent introduces itself and confirms it's ready
  • Connection status is visible on your Agent Settings page
TIP

Telegram is optional but recommended. Without it, you'll still get weekly digest emails and can manage everything through the dashboard.

04

Your Agent Goes Live

Your dedicated agent deploys automatically within minutes — seeded with your business context, ICP, and content tone. Five KPI goals are automatically set based on your plan tier.

  • Agent status appears on your dashboard with a live heartbeat indicator
  • Default goals are seeded: leads/month, posts/week, blogs/month, engagement rate, emails/week
  • Your first content draft appears in your dashboard within hours
  • You receive a Telegram notification when your agent activates
TIP

Most clients see their first blog post draft within 6 hours of the agent going live.

05

Review Content in Your Dashboard

Log in to agentgrow.io/user/dashboard to see everything your agent has prepared. Pending approvals appear prominently — each with a category icon (blog, social, email, leads, SEO) and full content preview.

  • Blog posts show full drafts in a scrollable preview
  • Social captions show exact text with hashtags
  • Click Approve to publish or Request Changes to send revision notes
  • Your agent also sends approval notifications via Telegram
  • Track all five KPIs with progress bars, trend arrows, and 30-day sparkline charts
TIP

Every approval and rejection is tracked. Your agent automatically receives a FEEDBACK.md file summarizing what works, what doesn't, and your preferences — so quality improves with every review cycle.

06

Use the Content Calendar

The Content Calendar at agentgrow.io/user/calendar gives you a visual overview of all scheduled and published content across categories.

  • Month view shows color-coded dots per day — blog (blue), social (violet), email (amber), leads (lime), SEO (sky)
  • Week view shows a detailed 7-day row with item titles inline
  • Click any day to see all items scheduled for that date
  • Drag an approval to a different day to reschedule it
  • Add manual entries directly from the calendar with the Add Content button
  • Filter by category to focus on specific content types
TIP

Use the calendar to spot gaps in your publishing schedule and add manual entries where needed.

07

Track Your Prospect Pipeline

The Prospects page at agentgrow.io/user/prospects shows every lead your agent has captured and nurtured, pulled from your dedicated CRM database.

  • Pipeline funnel bar on your dashboard shows prospects by status at a glance
  • Full prospect list with search, status filtering, and stage filtering
  • Expandable cards reveal complete details: company, role, score, notes, tags
  • Pagination for large prospect lists
  • Click "View all" from the dashboard funnel to jump directly to the full list
TIP

Check the pipeline funnel on your dashboard daily — it updates in real time as your agent captures and qualifies leads.

08

Communicate Via Telegram

Your agent is reachable on Telegram for instructions, questions, and quick direction changes. You don't need to log into the dashboard for everything.

  • Receive instant notifications when new content is ready for approval
  • Get alerts when your agent's status changes (active, paused, idle)
  • Send instructions directly: "focus on Kubernetes content this week"
  • Ask questions: "what's our top performing blog post this month?"
TIP

Treat Telegram like a Slack channel with your content team. Short messages get fast results.

09

Monitor Weekly Digests & Goals

Every Monday you receive a comprehensive digest email summarizing the past week's performance. Your dashboard also shows live KPI tracking with sparkline charts.

  • Weekly digest includes: goal progress table with trend arrows, this-week stats, pipeline summary, and pending approvals
  • Five KPIs tracked per plan tier — Starter: 40 leads/mo, 15 posts/wk, 4 blogs/mo, 4% engagement, 20 emails/wk
  • Growth gets 2.5x targets; Enterprise gets 6.25x targets
  • Progress bars are color-coded: amber (<60%), blue (60–99%), lime (100%+)
  • 30-day sparkline charts show trends at a glance
TIP

The compounding effect starts after 4–6 weeks of consistent publishing. Your agent's FEEDBACK.md is updated in real time as you approve or reject content — by month 3 it has detailed data on what converts, and by year 1 it holds institutional knowledge no new hire could match.

10

Tune and Scale

As your agent runs, four feedback loops make it smarter every week. When you approve or reject content, the system generates a FEEDBACK.md file with your approval patterns, rejection reasons, and preferences — and syncs it to your agent's workspace within 60 seconds. CRM outcomes adjust prospecting. Engagement metrics guide topic selection. Even failed outreach improves future templates.

  • Every approval/rejection is aggregated into FEEDBACK.md with 30-day stats by category, recent wins, and rejection feedback
  • Your agent reads FEEDBACK.md before creating content — categories below 70% approval rate trigger automatic adjustment
  • Re-edit your CBO setup form anytime from Agent Settings in the dashboard
  • Tell the agent what's working via Telegram: "the Kubernetes posts are getting good traction, write more"
  • Upgrade your plan to unlock more platforms and higher output targets
  • The longer your agent runs, the harder it is to replace — it builds deep context that compounds
TIP

Rejection notes are the highest-value signal. When you reject content with a note like "too salesy, needs value-first approach," the agent receives that feedback directly and adjusts future content accordingly.

Common Questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes. The Starter plan offers a 7-day free trial — no credit card required. Sign up with just your name and email, get instant dashboard access, and upgrade to a paid plan before the trial ends to keep your agent running.

How long does setup take?

The setup form takes about 3 minutes. Your agent goes live within an hour of submission.

What does the dashboard show?

Your dashboard shows live agent status with a heartbeat indicator, five KPI goals with progress bars and sparklines, pending content approvals, this-week stats, a prospect pipeline funnel, and a chronological activity feed. It auto-refreshes every 30 seconds.

Do I need to approve every post?

Content appears in your approval queue on the dashboard and as a Telegram notification. You can approve or reject with notes. Review everything in the first two weeks, then switch to spot-checking as you build confidence in quality.

What happens if I don't like the content quality?

Use Request Changes with specific feedback. The agent adjusts. If quality isn't meeting your standard after a few revision cycles, the team steps in to recalibrate the agent's context.

How does the Telegram integration work?

After your agent goes live, you receive a pairing code via email. Enter it in Telegram to connect. Your agent operates in a private chat — only you and the agent. You can check connection status on your Agent Settings page.

What is the Content Calendar?

The Content Calendar gives you month and week views of all scheduled and published content, color-coded by category (blog, social, email, leads, SEO). You can drag approvals to reschedule them, click days to see details, and add manual entries.

How does the prospect CRM work?

Your agent captures and qualifies leads into a dedicated CRM database. The dashboard shows a pipeline funnel, and the Prospects page gives you searchable, filterable access to every lead with full details, scores, and tags.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately and your KPI targets scale automatically. You can adjust your business profile via Agent Settings, and activate new platform modules via the Integrations page.

What is the weekly digest email?

Every Monday morning, you receive an email summarizing your goal progress with trend arrows, the week's output stats, pipeline summary, and any pending approvals. It's a comprehensive snapshot without needing to open the dashboard.

Does the agent actually get smarter over time?

Yes — and this is implemented, not aspirational. Every time you approve or reject content, the system generates a FEEDBACK.md file with your 30-day approval stats by category, what works (recent approvals with your notes), and what doesn't (rejections with your feedback). This file is synced to your agent's workspace within 60 seconds. The agent reads it before creating any content. CRM outcomes adjust prospecting, engagement metrics guide topic selection, and outreach results improve templates. By month 3, it has detailed data on what converts. By year 1, it holds institutional knowledge no new hire could match.

What is the refund guarantee exactly?

Each plan has specific minimum deliverables (e.g., Starter: 36 blog posts + 60 social posts in 90 days). If your agent fails to meet these commitments, you receive a 100% refund. Full details and per-plan minimums are in our Refund Policy.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Email hire@gheware.com with the subject "Cancellation Request." Your agent will be decommissioned at the end of the current billing period. You can request a CSV export of your CRM/lead data before decommission. All data is permanently deleted within 30 days.
